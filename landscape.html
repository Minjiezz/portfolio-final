Landscapes

These are the landscape photos I took during my trip to Florida. When I saw the beautiful scenery, I was so immersed in the beautiful atmosphere that I recorded these moments with photos.I wish I could record my feelings in different places in this way. Maybe in the future when I see these photos, I can recall the feeling at that time. Photos can't keep time, but they can help us freeze the moment.

The first half of these photos come from a trip I went to Miami Beach on Christmas 2021. I recorded the sunset by the sea. The second half of these photos are from my trip to Sichuan, China, in the summer of 2021. I hope I can go to more places and record different scenery in the future.

Another hobby of mine is traveling, I hope I can have more time in the future, take pictures while traveling, and travel to more places. The world is like a book to me, and the more places we travel, the more deeply we read and experience the book. I can also use the way of travel, learn more about the world.
