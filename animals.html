Animals

I like animals, so animals are also one of my favorite subject of photography. I hope to use the camera to record some beautiful moments about animals. There are many cute animals in this world, and the animals in each place have their own characteristics. I hope to visit more places in the future, see more different animals, and record them with my camera.

Some of the photos on this page are from a trip I took to Orlando and Miami in late 2021. I saw a lot of different animals in Florida. My favorite animals are cats, so my cats are one of my favorite subjects. I like to meet different cats in different places. I hope to use light, shadow and colors to meet a different world in the lens.

If you are also interested in animal photography, please come to me and explore how to take better animal photography. And if you have a cat, or any other kind of animal, I'd love to know how you interact with your pets.
